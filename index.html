About this site

We're first home builders and wanted to share what our build process has actually been like. Not the polished marketing version, but the real one.

Building a home has been very imperfect and very long. It's a roller coaster of emotions: excitement, anxiety, disappointment, and everything in between.

Disclaimer: Everything shared on this site is based on our real, personal experience. It may or may not be the same experience for everyone else.

One of the reasons we created this site is because we didn't have anyone recently who could walk us through the build process from a buyer's perspective, start to finish. No close friends or family we could sit down with and ask, ‚ÄúWhat was it really like?‚Äù

Hopefully, having a place to read about our journey helps someone who's in the same position we were, building for the first time without much firsthand guidance.

The posts here are written in timeline order. I started creating this site in February 2026, so I had to go back through emails and documents to capture past events accurately.

This site simply documents our personal experience. It doesn't provide financial advice, and it isn't intended to criticise or promote any builder, only to share what happened from our point of view.
