Searching for a New Builder

15 January 2026 · Builder

Key takeaways

  • Planning ahead means nothing if the builder doesn't follow through.
  • Communication is just as important as price and inclusions.
  • Transparent pricing makes decision-making much easier.
  • Being prepared before meetings leads to better outcomes.
  • The right consultant can make a huge difference.

By mid-January 2026, nothing had progressed with our pre-construction, even though everything had been planned months in advance. This was incredibly disappointing, especially given how clear and organised we had been with our timelines.

On top of that, communication from the builder had been consistently disappointing. Follow-ups were slow, expectations weren't met, and there was little confidence that things would improve.

Deciding to move on

At this point, we made the decision to look for a different builder, one that aligned better with our top priority: clear and consistent communication.

We went back to researching builders that might be a better fit for us and remembered that we had previously missed a meeting with La Vida Homes due to unforeseen circumstances. This time, we wanted to properly explore them as an option.

Researching La Vida Homes

We spent time reviewing what La Vida Homes had to offer, including their building inclusions, pricing, and overall approach.

What immediately stood out was their transparency. Prices were clear, and their tiered inclusions model made it easy to understand what was included at each level and choose what suited our budget and needs.

Preparing before the meeting

We reviewed their available floor plans and shortlisted the designs that worked best for us. From there, we started redesigning certain areas to better suit our lifestyle so we could go into the meeting well prepared.

Wanting to get a feel for how they operated, I booked a phone meeting directly through their website to speak with a sales consultant about our situation, timelines, and how their organisation runs builds.

A refreshing experience

From the very first interaction, the difference was noticeable. Our sales consultant communicated clearly, was well organised, straight to the point, and followed through on what was discussed.

That alone gave us confidence that this builder could be a much better fit for us as first home builders.

If you're planning to build, communication should never be an afterthought.
